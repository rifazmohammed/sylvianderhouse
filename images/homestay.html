WATER, THE MIRROR OF THE WORLD
(December 21, 2012 - March 21, 2013)

Opened in December 2012 after 7 years of construction, silence and privacy remain the "luxury" of Sylviander House, situated in the outskirts of the southern city of Alapuzha, known as the "Venice of the East". This Green Building is surrounded by a beautiful garden and ponds around. The structures are built with natural material 'kummayam' (Lime Mortar), antique wooden windows and traditional wooden doors which keep the rooms airy and cool. And inside, the traditional Kerala-style bathrooms and spacious verandas create an ambience of olden times, when the nature and humans were in perfect balance.

Vision
For several years we were searching for a place, where we could live and work as artists, where there would be enough space to welcome our guests; a place where we gather like-minded people for creative activities, for exchanging ideas, for building intercultural bridges or just simply to relax and enjoy good things life. Our search for a place to live started from the south of France, Sicily in Italy. Soon we realised in Europe we cannot 'afford' to such common vision. We continued our search in Alexander's birthplace in Kerala, South-India. And we finally found a land at Chettikad near Alapuzha. The true beauty of this place made both of us say - Yes! This is it!

Location Away from noise and pollution, Sylviander House is placed in the idyllic village landscape in Chettikad, 5kms north of Alapuzha; around 70kms from Kochi international airport at Nedumbassery.
